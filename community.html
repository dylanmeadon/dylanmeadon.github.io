Building Product Design communities from the ground up.

Through my company, Bakery Digital, I established and grew the largest community of aspiring product designers in South Africa at the time. This initiative connected hundreds of professionals, providing a platform for sharing knowledge, collaborating on projects, and fostering professional growth. Organizing meetups, workshops, and webinars, I created an environment where designers could learn from each other, stay updated on industry trends, and build meaningful connections.

Beyond just networking, I focused on creating a supportive and inclusive community. By mentoring emerging designers and facilitating discussions on best practices, tools, and techniques, I helped elevate the overall skill level within the community. This effort not only strengthened individual careers but also contributed to the broader design ecosystem in South Africa.
